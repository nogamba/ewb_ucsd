Our Information

Our organization sends out periodic newsletters to our members. If you would like to be on the mailing list for these newsletters, you can contact any of our officers via the facebook page (Facebook: Engineers Without Borders - UCSD) and they can add you. Also, coming to one of our events or a GBM is a great way to get to know the officers and other members better (as well as putting your email into our sign in sheet for ensured addition to the list). We welcome you to join us!
