What your support helps us do

Your support allows our organization to provide engineering students at UCSD with a mulitfacted experience while they participte in humanitarian aid. Our members are able receive hands on engineering experience through Project Teams as well as through the many Technical Skill Workshops we offer. All together, our diverse group of students brings creativity and a willingness to help others. This added with technical skills learned while in the organization allows our teams to think of creative and useful projects to help people all around the world who are in need. Our teams work relentlessly to build and complete their projects, while fostering an atmosphere of camraderie and learning throughout the teams and the organization. Your support makes all of this possible, thank you.

Toycar workshop

Here is a student preparing his toycar from pieces he laser cut, using a design from Solidworks. This workshop was designed to teach members how to use a laser cutter and design software to create a wind up toycar.

Solidworks Workshop

Here is a picture of part of the group of students who came to this workshop designed to teach students how to use solidworks. It was a multistep workshop series that allowed students to gain valuable experience on this software.

Sustain-a-thon 2019

This student and his team pitched an idea for self serve detergent dispensers in UCSD's laundry rooms, which would eliminate plastic waste created by all students having to use individual detergents. This team won second place.
