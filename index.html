Who are we?

It is our firm belief here at the UCSD-Chapter of EWB that meeting a basic standard of living should not be seen as a privilege, but rather as a basic human right. And we are committed to working toward a future where such phrases as â€˜clean drinking water' have become a relic of the past.

Therefore: our mission here at UCSD is to bring passionate students together to tackle projects aimed at improving the conditions of local and international communities in need with an emphasis on the sustainability of our efforts.

These projects are complex and require multidisciplinary effort, providing students with the opportunity to develop critical thinking, problem solving, and technical skills they will find applicable in any future field of study, industry, or volunteer work all while enacting real positive change during their time at UCSD.
