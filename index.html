Our Mission

It is our firm belief here at the UCSD-Chapter of EWB that meeting a basic standard of living should not be seen as a privilege, but rather as a basic human right. And we are committed to working toward a future where such phrases as "clean drinking water" have become a relic of the past. Therefore, our mission here at UCSD is to bring passionate students together to tackle projects aimed at improving the conditions of local and international communities in need with an emphasis on the sustainability of our efforts. These projects are complex and require multidisciplinary effort, providing students with the opportunity to develop critical thinking, problem solving, and technical skills they will find applicable in any future field of study, industry, or volunteer work all while enacting real positive change during their time at UCSD.

Who are we?

Engineers Without Borders USA builds a better world through engineering projects that empower communities to meet their basic human needs. We work with communities to find appropriate solutions for their infrastructure needs.

Engineers Without Borders is an organization dedicated to bringing together engineers from all paths of study to partner in working towards projects that aid people in all walks of life and all over the world. EWB-UCSD allows students to undertake leadership roles, learn new skills from all engineering fields, and teaches students to work in diverse teams. The goal of this club is to create sustainable projects that aid people in the community and around the world as well. These projects test critical thinking skills and help prepare students to face the challenges they will encounter in the future. Engineers Without Borders is dedicated to finding sustainable ways to help those who are most in need through these projects.
